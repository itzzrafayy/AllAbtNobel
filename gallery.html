Awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect. Born In March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany

Marie Curie

Received An Nobel Prize In Physics (1903) and Nobel Prize in Chemistry (1911), becoming the first woman to win a Nobel Prize and the first person to win two Nobel Prizes in different fields.
